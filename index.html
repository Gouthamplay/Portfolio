About Me

I'm a Design Lead and VFX Supervisor at Sheshi Pvt. Ltd, Bangalore — with 6+ years of experience that spans the gap between cinematic VFX production and product design. That cross-industry range isn't accidental; it's the thing I've built deliberately.

My time at Future Age Studio (4 years) was immersive: motion graphics for 100+ Kannada films, TVC campaigns for real-estate and consumer brands, lyrical videos, and VFX compositing for tricky shots in Maya and Unreal. The speed and volume of that environment taught me to solve visual problems fast without losing craft.

Since April 2024 at Sheshi, I've led UI/UX, motion, and brand design simultaneously — including directing a full app redesign of 300+ screens in a single month. I work well at the intersection of creative direction and hands-on execution. When I lead, I'm still in the files.
